THE UAJ SPORTS APP

What is it?

The UAJ sports app is your one stop locale for all things sports related. All you need to do is tell your Google Assitant capable device, "Talk to UAJ Sports," and you can hear headlines about PAC-12 teams, hear facts about events that happened today in sports history, or challenge your friends to a quiz about little known college sports facts. Add it to your Google actions library today!
What can it do?
Once you start the application on your device, you will have access to all of these awesome features!
QUIZ Want to show your friends just how much more sports trivia you know than them? Then use this feature to play this ten question quiz that keeps score with your friends. Now you will never have to argue again about who knows the most about obscure college sports facts. Be sure to come back to try your best on new questions every week!

NEWS Tired of having to open your phone every morning to find out what is going on with your favorite PAC-12 team? With this UAJ Sports App feature, all you need to do is listen. You can select any team in the NCAA PAC-12 conference to hear news about. You can also choose individual sports such as football and basketball within each team if you truly are a die-hard fan that needs to keep up with their favorite team!

Today in History Who won that big game thorty years ago on this day? What athlete did something amazing on this day in history? Did O.J. Simpson do something noteworthy on this day? All of the answers to these questions and more can be discovered through the Today in Hisotry feature on the UAJ sports app. Just remmeber, no day is the same so be sure to ask your Google Assitant to open UAJ sports everyday so you can learn a little bit about why today matters in sports history.
